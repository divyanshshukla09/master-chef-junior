➤ Take a baking container and place a butter paper on the bottom of the container if butter paper is not available then you have to place the ghee all around the container .
➤ In another container Add 2 Cup of all purpose flour(Maida)then add 1 spoon cocoa powder, 1 tspoon baking sodaand vanilla or rose assence. Add milk to it then add melted butter.
➤ If you are a vegetarian so you have to add 2 spoon condesed milk and if you are not a vegetarian so you have to add 2 egg. Add chocolate chips and dry fruit in it.
➤Now you have to mix all the items till the all items will convert into a smooth batter.
➤ Then store this batter in room temperature for 5 to 10 minutes.
➤Once your batter is ready you have to place whole batter into the container in which you have placed the butter paper or ghee.
➤ Now switch on your microwave and bake the batter for 5 minutes on 200 ℃
➤ Now take the cake out of the microwave and take out the butter paper from the cake.
➤ In last garnish the cake by chocolate chipe and white chocolate chips.
